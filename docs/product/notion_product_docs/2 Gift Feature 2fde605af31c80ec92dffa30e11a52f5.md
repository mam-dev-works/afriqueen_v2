# 2. Gift Feature

SEND GIFTS (from profile thumbnail or profile details)

Choose the gift you want to send

Rose = you have 4 roses left
, your roses will recharge in 8 hours (at midnight user's country time)
you can send a maximum of 5 roses per day
Chocolate = you have 0 chocolate left
, your chocolates will be refilled in 12 hours (24 hours after sending the previous chocolate) you can send
a maximum of 1 chocolate per day
Flower Bouquet = you have 0 Flower Bouquets left your Flower Bouquet will recharge in 32 hours (72 hours after sending the previous Flower
Bouquet) you can send a maximum
of 1 Flower Bouquet every 72 hours
Clothing = you have 0 Clothing left Your Clothing will be recharged in 3 days (Monday at midnight, user's country time) You can send a maximum
of 1 Clothing
per week
Heart = you have 1 Heart left your Heart will recharge in 23 days (the first of the month at midnight in the user's country) you can send a
maximum of 1
Heart per month
ring = you have 1 ring left your ring will be recharged in 90 days (90 days after sending the previous ring) you can send a maximum of 1 ring every 90
days

If you click on one of the buttons (rose, chocolate, bouquet, clothing, heart or ring):
-if there are still gifts available (example heart) this opens a confirmation window "are you sure you want to send a heart yes / no) if click on yes (confirmation message "your gift has been sent) and returns , it sends a heart
to the previous page, if click on no, returns to the previous page
-if no gifts are available (e.g. flower bouquet) opens information message “you do not have a flower bouquet available, your flower bouquet will be recharged in 32 hours”
Add a “back” button that takes you back to the previous page
